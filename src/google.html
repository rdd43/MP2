The Rise of Google

Authored By: Robert Diasio

What would be a website about the most influential websites if it did not include Google? Virtually inescapable, Google has become the Disney of the internet. But, it was not always like this. There was a time when Google was simply an idea in the heads of Larry Page and Sergey Brin.

In today's age, this is easy to see first hand. Simply go to Google.com and just type in "Google's mission statement." This may seem a bit redundant, right? Well, not really. If you think about it, Google has accomplished something that changed the world forever. The ability to look up anything at any time and get an answer that is (almost always) correct.

It is no wonder that when people think of the "Big Five" they think of Google at the top. Currently, Google resides as the No.1 Most visited website.

The Old Age Of The Internet

Before the 90s Search Engines were simply an idea. As a user of the internet, either you knew enough sites to get around, or you just have to fine out the old fashioned way, through books, forums or just word of mouth.

Google changed the way that people think of search engines. No longer do people need to wonder of how to find a piece of information or go searching in books for most information. All thanks to Google.
