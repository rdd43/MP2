Amazon and Online Shopping

Authored By: Robert Diasio

Founded in 1996 by Jeff Bezos, Amazon capitalized on the fact that many people just simply do not want to leave their house when shopping for goods, or in some cases, cannot find an item they need in the stores around them. For a while Amazon seemed to be one of the only sites where online shopping of this sort was possible. Then, in the early thousands, eBay came along and snagged the spot of being the No.1 online shopping website (peaking at No.4).

Come 2010 it can be seen that Amazon broke into the Top 5 most visited websites. No longer was eBay the most prevalent. Although, eBay still had a niche. However, when looking at the numbers, there is no denying that Amazon was more successful than any other wanna-be competitor. Sure, Amazon may not be in the top 10 most visited websites currently, but does that really matter when you are Jeff Bezos? Of course not. Bezos made his fortune off of Amazon's success, and while he may not be the most respectable man in the world, he is definitely one of the brightest men out there when it comes to money.

First Ever TRILLIONAIRE?

It is reported that by 2026 Bezos might actually become the worlds first trillionaire. That is $1,000,000,000,000. A feat that no one else has accomplished.
