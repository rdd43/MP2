The Effect on Business

Authored By: Robert Diasio

With the internet and websites becoming such a popular medium, there is no denying that there was an effect on how businesses run and operate. Some businesses have their entire model structured around working with the internet and having a great website. Just look at Google, Facebook, Amazon. If it was not for their website then these three titans of the corporate world would not exist.

Many other companies tried to emulate the success found by other pioneers, utilizing the internet and capitalizing on the market space that was there. It just so happened that Microsoft is not only one of these pioneers, but also a company that capitalized on the space that they found.

Many businesses use Microsoft related products in their day to day workflow. Microsoft office, outlook, word, and who can forget excel? Microsoft not only took advantage of the market that they found, but they also revolutionised the business world with the products and services that they delivered.

Working in Accounting or Finance?

Anyone working in accounting or finance can and should be thanking Microsoft. Not only has Microsoft created what is arguably the most important program to associates working in the accounting field, but because of the visual basic integration to the program workers can create programs to increase workflow.
